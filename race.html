Vastaya
The Vastaya are a chimeric race of Runeterra that are the weaker magical descendants from enlightened mortals that took the power of the spirit realm into themselves. Vastaya can look considerably different from one another, but they all share animal and human characteristics.

Creature Type

Humanoid. Lean and agile, your main characteristic is the endearing raccoon features that adorn your appearance. The ringed tail following you wherever you go and the dark circles that envelop your eyes.

Size

Bearing the slippery figure of a thief, your size varies between small and medium (your choice). (Medium)

Speed

Your experience in heists and running away from dire situations has led your kind to be quick on its feet, resulting in a speed faster than most creatures.
Your speed is 35 ft.

One ability score of your choice increases by 2. (Dexterity)

Feat

You gain one feat of your choice for which you qualify. (Mobile)

Variable Trait

Language
